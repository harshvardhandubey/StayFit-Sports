Summer Vacation cricket coaching camps for mumbai cricket for various age groups starting from 15th April 2017

As per the official website of mumbai cricket, www.mumbaicricket.com , The selection trials for the Summer Vacation Coaching Nets of the Mumbai Cricket Association will be held on Friday, 15th April 2017 at all Centres. The candidates are required to go to the Centre nearest to their place of residence in white cricket clothing.

Fees - 10000 Rs for 2 Months
