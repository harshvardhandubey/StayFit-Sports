Summer Vacation VolleyBall coaching camps for mumbai VolleyBall for various age groups starting from 20th April 2017

As per the official website of mumbai FootBall, www.mumbaivba.com , The selection trials for the Summer Vacation Coaching Nets of the Mumbai VolleyBall Association will be held on Friday, 15th April 2017 at all Centres. The candidates are required to go to the Centre nearest to their place of residence in white clothing.

Fees - 10000 Rs for 2 Months

We take great pleasure informing member schools, that our Association will be conducting the Sixteenth Free Training–Cum–Coaching Camp Under Sir Dorabji Tata Trust-Sports promotion Fund for Girls only, between the age group 8 to 16 years from 20th April 2015 to 8th May 2015 from 8.00 am to 9.30 am in Football, Throwball, Handball, Volleyball, and Tennis at the MSSA-Castrol Schools Sports Centre, Azad Maidan (North End), Mahapalika Marg, Mumbai – 400 001.

This training-Cum-Coaching Camp is especially held in order to help the Girls Students of our member schools to enhance their performance and also enrich their knowledge in their respective discipline of sports. Eminent coaches connected with coaching school children will be in charge of the Camp.
The Association is trying to fulfill the dream of Sir Dorabji Tata the initiator of the Association, with financial help coming from Sir. Dorabji Tata Trust –Sports Promotion Fund. Member schools are requested to take advantage of the Coaching Camp by sending their School Girls as the Camp is Free of Cost. A nominal registration fee of Rs.100/- will be charged per student.

Head of the schools willing to send their Girls Students for the camp are requested to send the names of the students along with the Dates of Birth and Class on the school letterhead along with a fee of Rs.100/- per student to the Association by 10th April, 2015.
Those students whose names are submitted for the Camp must report at the MSSA- Castrol Schools Sports Centre, Azad Maidan (North End), Mahapalika Marg, Mumbai – 400 001, on 20th April 2015 , i.e., 10 minutes prior to the schedule time of the Camp.
Kindly send the names on the school letterhead with the name of the game mentioned which the student would like to attend.
